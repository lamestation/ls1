# Step 6: Noise Filter

**R35** and **C11** form a noise filter for the output from the
amplifier.

## Tools Needed

- Soldering iron
- Cutter

## Parts Needed

- 1 x 100Ω resistor

  ![](images/15302728.jpg)

- 1 x 0.1μF capacitor

  ![](images/15302729.jpg?width=250)

## Instructions

1.  Solder the 100Ω resistor into **R35**.

    ![](images/15302736.jpg?width=500)

2.  Solder the 0.1μF capacitor into **C11**.
